hi, you can call me jp!

i'm a canadian-based, chronically online rabbit, self-taught multi-genre musician and producer, ADHD-haver, and game developer with amniotic band syndrome pursuing a variety of creative endeavours!

a "jack of all trades" or "interdisciplinary artist" or whatever, i've always had a great appreciation for many kinds of art in many kinds of mediums as well as a love for learning and creating. you may see many projects with disparate tones and styles, often consecutively, but no matter what, my goal is always to be honest in my art, while not taking myself too seriously—because art should be fun after all ;) i hope you enjoy your stay!

the website

i created this site initially as a simple hub for all of my projects and commission information but over time it's shifted to being a very personal creative outlet for me out of a growing frustration with the modern centralised internet and social media as well as the discovery of the Web Revival.

i'm roughly following the principles of brutalist web design (though, i tend to play it loose when it comes to decoration because i just can't help myself!) and i try to adhere to the Accessibility Developer Guide! (and if you have any issues in terms of accessibility feel free to message me about it!)

this site was coded by hand using Neovim, proudly hosted on Neocities, and deployed with deploy-to-neocities. i use the BEM CSS methodology as well as Sass for styling.

you can find the source code for this site here (it's nothing special though, trust me lol).
