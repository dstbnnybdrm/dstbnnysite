hi, you can call me jp !
i'm a canadian-based, chronically online rabbit, self-taught multi-genre musician and producer, ADHD-haver, and learning game developer, pursuing a variety of creative endeavors !

a "jack of all trades" or "interdisciplinary artist" or whatever, i've always had a great appreciation for many kinds of art in many kinds of mediums as well as a love for learning and creating. you may see many projects with disparate tones and styles, often consecutively, but no matter what, my goal is always to be honest in my art, and not take myself too seriously doing it.
