tabs

recently i started writing out guitar tabs for songs that i love but didn’t have any at the time. it’s been great practice for my ears and also weirdly fun and almost meditative? i figure them out by ear, close investigative examinations of live videos (if i can find any), or sometimes dissecting them within a DAW.

i’ve been posting them on Ultimate Guitar Tabs but i figured i might as well put them here too! click on a link to view it in another tab, or you can right-click and “save link as…” to download the text file for yourself!
