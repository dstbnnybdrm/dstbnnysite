mixing

price

  • up to 4 tracks : $50 + $5 per additional track
  • if tuning requested : $10 + $5 per additional track to tune

description

a full mixdown of an entire song; can be either vocal or instrumental ex. multiple vocal tracks and one stereo instrumental, individual instrument stems (drums, bass, guitar, synths, etc.). the initial mix will be given to you, at which point revisions can be requested and made (major revisions may result in a price increase, however, i will otherwise be lenient with this).

deliverables

once you are satisfied the following deliverable(s) will be provided to you :

  • the final mix as a 44.1k 24b .wav file
  • (if applicable and requested) an instrumental version of the mix as a 44.1k 24b .wav file*
  • (if applicable and requested) a TV track** version of the mix as a 44.1k 24b .wav file

*ONLY if the instrumental was NOT provided to me as a single audio file
**the mix with the background vocals/harmonies intact but no lead vocals

please provide

  • each track to be mixed as audio files, preferably as 44.1k/48k 24b .wav files, named accordingly to their content
  • not mandatory, but one or more reference songs that match the overall vibe that you want to capture would be very helpful !

mastering

price

  • mastering : $25
  • alongside a full mixing service : discounted to $20

description

mastering a final mix to your desired loudness and the specifications of most streaming services, or your own specifications if provided. a preview of the final master will be given to you, at which point revisions can be requested and made (major revisions may result in a price increase).

deliverables

once you are satisfied the following deliverable(s) will be provided to you :

  • the final master as a 44.1k 24b .wav file
  • the final master as a 44.1k 24b .wav file to be used on social media sites*
  • the final master as a 320kbps .mp3 file
  • (if requested) the final master as a 44.1k 16b .wav file

*True Peaks lowered to around -3dbTP, to minimize distortion issues from audio compression (i.e that meme-y "deep-fried" sound)

please provide

  • the final mix to be mastered, as a 44.1k/48k 24b .wav file, properly named
