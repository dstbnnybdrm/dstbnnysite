audio editing

in the case where only a single or few tracks need to be processed/polished, ex. tuning and timing vocals for a song, correcting the timing on guitar doubles, general vocal processing for voice over, etc. the initial result will be given to you, at which point revisions can be requested and made.

price

  • tuning: $15
  • timing: $5
  • general processing (corrective eq, compression, etc.): ~$10

deliverables

once you are satisfied the following deliverable(s) will be provided to you:

  • the processed audio as 44.1kHz 16-bit .wav file(s)

please provide

  • the file(s) to be processed, named accordingly to their content.

mixing

a full mixdown of an entire song; can be either vocal or instrumental ex. multiple vocal tracks and one stereo instrumental, individual instrument stems (drums, bass, guitar, synths, etc.). the initial mix will be given to you, at which point revisions can be requested and made (major revisions may result in a price increase, however, i will otherwise be lenient with this).

price

  • up to 4 tracks: $50 + $5 per additional track
  • if tuning requested: $10 + $5 per additional track to tune

deliverables

once you are satisfied the following deliverable(s) will be provided to you:

  • the final mix as a 44.1kHz 16-bit .wav file
  • (if applicable and requested) an instrumental version of the mix as a 44.1k 16-bit .wav file*
  • (if applicable and requested) a TV track** version of the mix as a 44.1k 16-bit .wav file

*ONLY if the instrumental was NOT provided to me as a single audio file
**the mix with the background vocals/harmonies intact but no lead vocals

please provide

  • each track to be mixed as audio files, preferably as 44.1k/48k 16-bit (or higher) .wav files, named accordingly to their content.
  • not mandatory, but one or more reference songs that match the overall vibe that you want to capture would be very helpful!

mastering

mastering a final mix to your desired loudness and the specifications of most streaming services, or your own specifications if provided. a preview of the final master will be given to you, at which point revisions can be requested and made (major revisions may result in a price increase).

price

  • mastering: $25
  • alongside a full mixing service: discounted to $20

deliverables

once you are satisfied the following deliverable(s) will be provided to you:

  • the final master as a 44.1k 16-bit .wav file
  • the final master as a 44.1k 16-bit .wav file to be used on social media sites*
  • the final master as a 320kbps .mp3 file
  • (if requested) the final master in a different, specified file format

*True Peaks lowered to around -3dbTP, to minimize distortion issues from audio compression (i.e that meme-y "deep-fried" sound)

please provide

  • the final mix to be mastered, as a 44.1k/48k 16-bit (or higher) .wav file, properly named.

please read the terms of service in its entirety if considering ordering a commission, thank you!

terms of service

  • all commissions must be paid upfront.
  • work on the commission will not begin until payment is received.
  • all prices listed are subject to change depending on the complexity of the song (length, amount of tracks, etc.).
  • if something has happened on my end in which i am ultimately unable to deliver the commision you will be notified and refunded accordingly. otherwise all commissions are non-refundable.
  • i reserve the right to deny any order before payment occurs.
  • because revisions are allowed i am not responsible for any dissatisfaction with the final product.

order

to order a commission you can send me an email at dustbunny.bedroom@gmail.com with the following information:

  • your name/artist name.
  • the type of service you would like to commission (audio editing, mixing, or mastering).
  • any particular notes or considerations regarding your commissions.

once contact begins (i.e i respond) i may request that you send ALL the files that you wish to be processed. from here we can discuss payment and the details of your commission more.
