hi, you can call me jp!
i'm a canadian-based, chronically online rabbit, self-taught multi-genre musician and producer, ADHD-haver, and game developer, pursuing a variety of creative endeavours!

a "jack of all trades" or "interdisciplinary artist" or whatever, i've always had a great appreciation for many kinds of art in many kinds of mediums as well as a love for learning and creating. you may see many projects with disparate tones and styles, often consecutively, but no matter what, my goal is always to be honest in my art, while not taking myself too seriously—because art should be fun after all ;) i hope you enjoy your stay!

and for any inquiries you can send me an email at: dustbunnybedroommusic@gmail.com

the website

the dstbnnysite was coded by hand using neovim and deployed with deploy-to-neocities.

the dstbnnyblog was built using zonelets and the posts themselves are written in markdown using zero-md.

i'm roughly following the principles of brutalist design (i tend to play it loose when it comes to decoration) and use the BEM CSS methodology for my class names.
