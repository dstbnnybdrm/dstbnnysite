commissions

please read the terms of service before ordering a commission <3

vocal mixing (price: $40-$70)

i will mix your vocals into an instrumental provided by you !

  • can include up to four tracks for doubles, harmonies, adlibs, etc.
    • (more may cost an additional fee)
  • includes vocal tuning, timing, or any other desired effects and manipulations
  • additionally, i can also edit the instrumental a bit too to a reasonable degree
    • (adding fades, extending/cutting sections, limiting/compression, etc.)

stem mixing (price: $40-$80)

if you provide individual stem tracks for your song i will mix them down into a final mix that is ready for mastering !

  • either vocal or instrumental songs
  • generally involves: corrective EQ, compression/limiting, balancing, reverb/delay, etc.

mastering (price: $20-$40)

i will master a given song to your desired loudness and to the specifications of most streaming services !

  • i can also provide a separate master to be used for promotional material on social media platforms (twitter, instagram, etc.)
    • this is to avoid "deepfrying"/clipping that these sites might cause due to their file encoding and compression
