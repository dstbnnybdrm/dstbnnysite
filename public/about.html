hi, you can call me jp!

i'm a canadian-based, chronically online rabbit, self-taught multi-genre musician and producer, ADHD-haver, and game developer with amniotic band syndrome pursuing a variety of creative endeavours!

a "jack of all trades" or "interdisciplinary artist" or whatever, i've always had a great appreciation for many kinds of art in many kinds of mediums as well as a love for learning and creating. you may see many projects with disparate tones and styles, often consecutively, but no matter what, my goal is always to be honest in my art, while not taking myself too seriously—because art should be fun after all ;) i hope you enjoy your stay!

the website

dstbnnybdrm, as well as the dstbnnyblog, was coded by hand using neovim and deployed with deploy-to-neocities.

i'm roughly following the principles of brutalist design (i tend to play it loose when it comes to decoration) and i use the BEM CSS methodology for my class names as well as Sass.
