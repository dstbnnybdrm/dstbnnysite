stifled screaming

there is a want deep inside me. it starts as a dull pain in the centre of my body. it bites & claws at the bones of its cage. it yearns to grow louder & louder like a stifled scream. suddenly i feel it in every atom. throbbing & shaking like heart palpitations. but the screaming doesn't come.

i wish i could love you fully;

i wish i could have what you have;

i wish i could steal yr skin for myself.
