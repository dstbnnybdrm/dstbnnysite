interests

music

i like to think that i have a pretty broad music taste and will probably enjoy listening to most things if they're done well, with sincerity and intention, or simply just interestingly. but of course i definitely do gravitate more towards a select few of my favourite genres/scenes, the ones that i find have had the most influence on me as a creator and otherwise.

emo (and emo-adjacent)

i love and appreciate all the different waves of emo, all the different subgenres, and musically i'm very, very inbedbted to the genre; despite the many different styles i work in i think there's always at least a little bit of emo influence present in my work.

i first got into emo around the time of the emo revival with bands like Modern Baseball and Marietta

then, as every hyperfixating neurodivergent knows, in my growing interest of the genre i wanted to learn more about the history of the genre, listening to ... like American Football and Cap 'n Jazz' of the midwest, and Sunny Day Real Estate and Mineral.

even though it's become a bit of a meme somehow i love the direction the genre and community is going in lately with the "fifth wave".
